Campus Marketplace

Description Of Application

The Campus Marketplace App (CMA) is a web-based platform designed for members of the Kwantlen Polytechnic University (KPU) community. The system provides a secure and safe online marketplace where students, faculty, and staff can buy and sell items.

The application enables users to:

  • Register and log in using only their KPU credentials.
  • Create, edit, and manage product listings.
  • Search and browse available items.
  • Communicate with buyers and sellers through an internal messaging system.

The system aims to improve accessibility, convenience, and safety compared to public online marketplaces by restricting access to only verified KPU community members.
